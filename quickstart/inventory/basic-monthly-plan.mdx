This document provides a step-by-step guide on how to set up your first plan in Nexudus. Plans represent services customers subscribe to, and they usually represent your coworking memberships or private offices inventory. Plans can include benefits such as booking or printing credit, which will be assigned to customers signing up to them.

Some examples of basic plans include:

*   **Monthly Hot Desk:** Access to any available desk in a shared area.
*   **Dedicated Desk:** A permanent desk in a shared space, often with lockable storage.
*   **Private Office:** Lockable, fully furnished office for teams or individuals.
*   **Virtual Office:** Business address + mail handling + limited on-site access
*   **Evening / Weekend Access:** Discounted or specialized access for non-standard hours.
*   **Team Hot Desk / Office Packages**
*   **Hybrid Worker Plan:** e.g. 2â€“3 days/week office access.

Follow the steps below to create your first plan and streamline your coworking space management.

<Steps>
 <Step title="Navigate to Plans and Add a New Plan">
    1. Go to **Inventory** in the left-hand navigation menu.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_0_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on **Plans** to access the plans settings.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_0_1.gif"/>
    3. Click the **Add plan** button to start creating a new plan.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_0_2.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Select a Plan Type and Enter Plan Details">
    1. Choose a plan type. For this example, select **Manual entry**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_1_0.gif"/>
    2. Enter the **Plan name**. In this case, type &#x27;Dedicated Desk&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_1_1.gif"/>
    3. Add a **Plan description**. In this case, type &#x27;Dedicated Desk&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_1_2.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Set Pricing for the Plan">
    1. Click on the **Pricing** tab.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_2_0.gif"/>
    2. Enter the **Price** for the plan. For example, set it to &#x27;750&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_2_1.gif"/>
    3. Click **Save changes** to save the plan.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_2_2.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add Benefits to the Plan">
    1. Click on the **Dedicated Desk** plan to open its details.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_0.gif"/>
    2. Navigate to the **Benefits** tab.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_1.gif"/>
    3. Click on **Add money credit** to add a money credit benefit.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_2.gif"/>
    4. Enter a **Credit description**. For example, type &#x27;Meeting Room Credit&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_3.gif"/>
    5. Enter the **Credit amount**. For example, set it to &#x27;150&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_4.gif"/>
    6. Select the booking types this credit can be used for. Choose &#x27;This credit can only be used to pay the following types of resources&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_5.gif"/>
    7. Select the specific resources. For example, choose &#x27;Large Meeting Rooms&#x27; and &#x27;Small Meeting Rooms&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_6.gif"/>
    8. Click **Save changes** to save the money credit benefit.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3_7.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add Passes to the Plan">
    1. Click on the **Passes** tab.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on **Add pass** to add a pass benefit.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_1.gif"/>
    3. Select a **Pass**. For example, choose &#x27;Day Pass&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_2.gif"/>
    4. Enter the **Amount** of passes. For example, set it to &#x27;7&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_3.gif"/>
    5. Set the **Expiration** for the passes. For example, choose &#x27;Every week&#x27;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_4.gif"/>
    6. Click **Save changes** to save the pass benefit.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4_5.gif"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
