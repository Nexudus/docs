A &#x27;Product&#x27; in the Nexudus Platform represents an item or service you sell to customers. Products can include one-off sales or recurring services with benefits, such as booking or printing credits. Setting up products involves configuring details like availability, pricing, and integrations. Best practices include using clear and descriptive names, setting appropriate pricing and availability, and utilizing integration options to enhance sales operations.

**Typical Uses:**

- Selling ad-hoc services or items.
- Offering subscriptions with added benefits.
- Displaying products in member portals and apps.

Products can be finely tuned to cater to different customer types and sales environments, supported by rich integration features with platforms like Square ePOS and NexKiosk.
