---
title: Setup Resources
description: Learn how to set up your first resource in Nexudus.
---

In Nexudus, **Resources** are spaces or items that can be booked via a calendar, such as meeting rooms, phone booths, studios, or equipment. This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your first resource, ensuring your coworking space can efficiently manage and offer bookable amenities to your members.

We will start by creating a **Resource Type**, then we will create a **Resource** and finally we will add a **Rate** to the resource.

<Steps>
 <Step title="Navigate to Resource Types">
    1. Navigate to the **Inventory** section in the left-hand menu.
    2. In the **Rooms and resources** section, click on **Resource types**.
    3. Click on the **+ Add resource type** button.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Create a Resource Type">
    1. In the **Type name** field, enter the name for the resource type. In this example, we will use &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms&#x60;.
    2. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Navigate to Resources">
    1. Navigate to the **Inventory** section in the left-hand menu.
    2. In the **Rooms and resources** section, click on **Resources**.
    3. Click on the **+ Add Resource** button.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Select Resource Type">
    1. Select the **Meeting Room** resource type.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_3.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_3.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_3.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Fill Resource Details">
    1. In the **Resource name** field, enter the name for the resource. In this example, we will use &#x60;Small Meeting Room 1&#x60;.
    2. In the **Resource type** field, select the resource type you created earlier. In this example, we will use &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms - Coworking Central Street&#x60;.
    3. In the **Category** field, enter the category for the resource. In this example, we will use &#x60;Meeting Rooms&#x60;.
    4. Enable the **Display this resource on the web portal and the members App** toggle.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_4.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_4.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_4.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add a Photo">
    1. Navigate to the **Features** tab.
    2. In the **Photo** section, click on the **Select file** button.
    3. Select the image you want to use for the resource.
    4. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_5.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_5.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_5.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add a Rate">
    1. Navigate to the **Rates** tab.
    2. Click on the **+ Add rate** button.
    3. In the **Price name** field, enter the name for the rate. In this example, we will use &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms Hourly Rate&#x60;.
    4. In the **Price** section, enter the price for the resource. In this example, we will use &#x60;$25 USD every hour&#x60;.
    5. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_6.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_6.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_6.png"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
