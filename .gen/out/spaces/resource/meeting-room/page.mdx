---
title: Setup First Resource
description: Set up your first resource.
---

Resources are any items or areas that can be booked via a calendar. This guide will show you how to set up your first resource in Nexudus.

Some examples of resources include:

- **Meeting rooms, Boardrooms and Conference rooms**
- **Phone booths or private call pods**
- **Photo/video studios**
- **Materials or tools in a Makerspace**
- **Music rooms or soundproof booths**
- **Lockers**
- **Parking spaces**
- **Mailboxes**
- **Storage units**
- **3D printers**

<Steps>
 <Step title="Create Resource Type">
    1. Navigate to **Inventory &gt; Rooms and Resources &gt; Resource types**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on the **Add resource type** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0_1.gif"/>
    3. In the **Type name** field, enter the name of the resource type. For example, type &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0_2.gif"/>
    4. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_0_3.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Create Resource">
    1. Navigate to **Inventory &gt; Rooms and Resources &gt; Resources**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on the **Add Resource** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_1.gif"/>
    3. Select the **Meeting Room** resource type.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_2.gif"/>
    4. In the **Resource name** field, enter the name of the resource. For example, type &#x60;Small Meeting Room 1&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_3.gif"/>
    5. In the **Resource type** field, select the resource type you created earlier. For example, select &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms - Coworking Central Street&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_4.gif"/>
    6. In the **Category** field, enter the category of the resource. For example, type &#x60;Meeting Rooms&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_5.gif"/>
    7. Click on the **Features** tab.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_6.gif"/>
    8. Click on the **Select file** button to upload a photo of the resource.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_7.gif"/>
    9. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_1_8.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add Rate">
    1. Select the resource you created. For example, select &#x60;Small Meeting Room 1&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on the **Rates** tab.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_1.gif"/>
    3. Click on the **Add rate** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_2.gif"/>
    4. In the **Price name** field, enter the name of the rate. For example, type &#x60;Small Meeting Rooms Hourly Rate&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_3.gif"/>
    5. In the **Price** field, enter the price of the resource. For example, type &#x60;25&#x60; and select &#x60;USD&#x60; and &#x60;every hour&#x60;.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_4.gif"/>
    6. Click on the **Save changes** button.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/spaces/resource/meeting-room/step_2_5.gif"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
