title: Setting Up Your First Plan

This documentation will guide you through the process of setting up your first plan in Nexudus. Plans represent services customers subscribe to, they usually represent your coworking memberships or private offices inventory. Plans can include benefits such as booking or printing credit, which will be assigned to customers signing up to them.

Some examples of basic plans:

*   **Monthly Hot Desk**: Access to any available desk in a shared area.
*   **Dedicated Desk**: A permanent desk in a shared space, often with lockable storage.
*   **Private Office**: Lockable, fully furnished office for teams or individuals.
*   **Virtual Office**: Business address + mail handling + limited on-site access
*   **Evening / Weekend Access**: Discounted or specialized access for non-standard hours.
*   **Team Hot Desk / Office Packages**
*   **Hybrid Worker Plan**: e.g. 2â€“3 days/week office access.

Let's get started!

<Steps>
 <Step title="Creating a New Plan">
    1. Navigate to the **Inventory** section in the left-hand menu, then click on **Plans**.
    2. Click on the **Add plan** button, then select **Manual entry** to create a plan from scratch.

    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_0.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Configuring Plan Details">
    1. In the **Plan details** tab, enter the **Plan name** and **Plan description**. For this example, we will create a plan called &#x27;Dedicated Desk&#x27;.
    2. Move to the **Pricing** tab.

    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_1.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Setting Up Pricing">
    1. In the **Pricing** tab, set the **Price** and **Frequency** for the plan. For example, set the price to $750 per month.
    2. Click on **Save changes** to save the plan.

    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_2.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Adding Plan Benefits">
    1. Open the plan you&#x27;ve just created and navigate to the **Benefits** tab.
    2. In the **Money credit** section, click on **Add money credit**.
    3. Enter a **Credit description** and **Credit amount**. For example, enter &#x27;Meeting Room Credit&#x27; and &#x27;$150&#x27;.
    4. Select the type of bookings this credit can be used for. For example, select &#x27;This credit can only be used to pay the following types of resources&#x27; and choose &#x27;Large Meeting Rooms&#x27; and &#x27;Small Meeting Rooms&#x27;.
    5. Click on **Save changes**.

    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_3.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Adding Passes">
    1. In the **Passes** section, click on **Add pass**.
    2. Select the **Pass** you want to add. For example, select &#x27;Day Pass&#x27;.
    3. Set the **Amount** of passes included in the plan. For example, set it to &#x27;7&#x27; passes a week to give members unlimited access.
    4. Set the **Expiration** for the passes. For example, select &#x27;Every week&#x27;.

    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/simple-plan/step_4.gif"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
