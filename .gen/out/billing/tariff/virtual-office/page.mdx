---
title: Create VO Plan
description: Create a virtual office plan, configure onboarding options, delivery handling, and associated charges.
---

Virtual office plans in Nexudus offer a way to manage customers who require services like mail handling and forwarding without needing a physical office space. This feature allows you to set up specific onboarding procedures, manage delivery options, and apply associated charges, making it ideal for coworking spaces looking to offer comprehensive virtual office solutions.

**Key features of virtual office plans include:**

*   **Customizable Onboarding:** Define identity and address verification processes, either manually or via Stripe Identity (if supported in your region).
*   **Delivery Handling:** Manage various delivery types such as mail, parcels, and checks, with options for storing, forwarding, scanning, and more.
*   **Charge Association:** Link specific products and charges to each delivery handling option.
*   **Alias and Recipient Limits:** Set limits for company aliases, additional recipients, and forwarding addresses.

<Steps>
 <Step title="Navigate and Add Plan">
    1. Navigate to the **Inventory** section in the side menu.
    2. Click on **Plans**.
    3. Click the **Add plan** button.
    4. Select **Manual entry** to create a new plan from scratch.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_0.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_0.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_0.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Fill in Plan Details">
    1. Enter **Virtual Office Basic** as the plan name.
    2. Enter **Virtual Office Basic** as the plan description.
    3. Enable the **This plan is a virtual office plan** toggle.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_1.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_1.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_1.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Configure Virtual Office Settings">
    1. Navigate to the **Virtual Office** tab.
    2. Enable the **Customers in this plan are required to provide proof of address** toggle.
    3. Select **Manual** for how you would like to check proof of address.
    4. Click on **Reuse previous list** to use a previously saved list of valid documents.
    5. Enable the **Customers in this plan are required to provide proof of ID** toggle.
    6. Select **Manual** for how you would like to check proof of ID.
    7. Click on **Reuse previous list** to use a previously saved list of valid documents.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_2.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_2.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_2.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Set Up Delivery Handling">
    1. Click on **Add delivery type(s)**.
    2. Select **Parcel** and **Checks** as delivery categories.
    3. Click on **Add handling option(s)**.
    4. For **Parcel**, change the delivery handling option to **Return to sender**.
    5. Set the maximum number of free storage days for **Collect** to **2**.
    6. Link the **Delivery Processing Charge** to the **Collect** handling option.
    7. Link the **Forwarding Charges** to the **Forward** handling option.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_3.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_3.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_3.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Define Company Limits">
    1. Set the maximum additional company aliases to **2**.
    2. Set the maximum additional recipients to **2**.
    3. Set the maximum forwarding addresses per recipient to **2**.
    4. Click **Save changes**.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_4.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_4.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_4.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Set Pricing and Save">
    1. Navigate to the **Pricing** tab.
    2. Set the price to **175** USD per month.
    3. Click **Save changes**.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_5.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_5.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_5.png"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Change Plan Name">
    1. Navigate to the **Plan details** tab.
    2. Change the plan name to **Virtual Office Standard**.
    3. Click **Save changes**.
    <img width="150" class="step_frame"
        data-png="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_6.png"
        data-gif="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_6.gif"
        src="/images/guides/frames/billing/tariff/virtual-office/step_6.png"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
