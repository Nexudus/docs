---
title: Add New Product
description: Learn how to set up your first product in Nexudus.
---

This guide shows you how to set up your first product in Nexudus. Products can be anything from coffee or snacks to office supplies or rental services. By adding products to your Nexudus account, you can easily manage your inventory and sales, and offer your members a wider range of services. 

Follow these steps to add a new product:

1.  Navigate to **Inventory** in the Admin Panel.
2.  Click on **Products**.
3.  Click on **Add product**.
4.  Fill in the product details, including name, description, and price.
5.  Save the product.

<Steps>
 <Step title="Navigate to Products">
    1. From the dashboard, navigate to the **Inventory** section on the left sidebar.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_0_0.gif"/>
    2. Click on **Products** to view the list of available products.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_0_1.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Add a New Product">
    1. Click the **Add product** button located on the top right of the screen.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_1_0.gif"/>
    2. Select **Manual entry** to create a new product from scratch.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_1_1.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Enter Product Details">
    1. In the **Product name** field, enter the name of the product. For example, type **Keycard Replacement Chart**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_2_0.gif"/>
    2. In the **Product description** field, enter a description of the product. For example, type **A charge for customers having lost their key cards**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_2_1.gif"/>
    3. In the **Unit price** field, enter the price of the product. For example, type **25**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_2_2.gif"/>
    4. In the **The position to display this product at** field, enter the position to display this product at. For example, type **8**.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_2_3.gif"/>
 </Step>
 <Step title="Save the Product">
    1. Click the **Save changes** button to save the new product.
    <img width="150" src="/images/guides/frames/billing/product/simple-product/step_3_0.gif"/>
 </Step>
</Steps>
